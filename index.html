Сoefficient of friction (f) ℹ

IS 11592-2000 (pg: 17)


Artificial friction coefficient (idler + belt resistance)

Standard Values:
• 0.020 → Normal conveyor
• 0.012 → Downhill conveyor (with brake)

Typical Range:
• 0.016 → Best conditions
• 0.020 → Standard (most cases)
• 0.025–0.030 → Poor conditions

f increases when:
• High friction material
• Trough angle > 30°
• Belt speed > 5 m/s
• Poor alignment / dusty / wet
• Low temperature (<20°C)
• Large idler spacing

f decreases when:
• Proper alignment
• Smooth idlers
• Clean & dry conditions

Notes:
• Standard value (0.020) valid for normal conditions
• f may go >0.030 in extreme cases (mines)
